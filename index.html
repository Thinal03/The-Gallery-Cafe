About Us

The Galery Cafe is one of the oldest and most reputed restaurants in Sri Lanka. The Galery Cafe is open daily from 11:30am to 3:00pm for lunch and 6:30pm to 11:00pm for dinner. While striving for perfect customer satisfaction, we also believe in offering authentic food that is created specifically for the local palate. We take pride in offering a diverse set of dishes from all provinces of Sri Lanka.

Its unique charm and unequalled dedication to the palate of the connoisseur have earned The Galery Cafe a reputation of being the very best. We use only the finest ingredients from Sri Lanka. Our seafood and meats are fresh, never frozen, upholding the highest standard of quality.

Indulge in Our Sumptuous Buffet

Experience the ultimate culinary journey at our restaurant's lavish buffet. Our chefs take pride in offering an extensive array of dishes, catering to every palate. From freshly prepared salads and artisanal bread to a variety of international cuisines, our buffet promises a feast for your senses. Start your meal with a selection of appetizers, move on to mouth-watering main courses, and finish with an irresistible assortment of desserts. Each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients, ensuring a delightful dining experience. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a delicious meal, our buffet offers something for everyone. Join us and savor the flavors of our diverse and delectable offerings.

Convenient Parking for Our Guests

We understand the importance of hassle-free parking when you visit our restaurant. Our dedicated parking area, located just steps from our entrance, ensures you can easily find a spot. For your convenience, we offer well-marked spaces, accessible parking, and clear signage to guide you. Additionally, during peak hours, our complimentary valet service is available to make your visit even smoother. Should our lot be full, we have partnered with nearby parking facilities to guarantee you always have a place to park. Relax and Unwind in Our Elegant Lounge

Step into our inviting lounge, where comfort meets sophistication. Perfect for both casual get-togethers and intimate gatherings, our lounge offers a cozy atmosphere with plush seating and ambient lighting.
